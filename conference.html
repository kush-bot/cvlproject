ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The conference will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results in theory, methodology, applications impacts and challenges of sustainable developments in Civil Engineering globally.

OBJECTIVES OF THE CONFERENCE

The focus of the conference is to disseminate useful information about the recent trends in Civil Engineering domain including sustainable development. The main objective of the conference is to bring together leading academicians, technologists, researchers, PG students and research scholars, to exchange and share their experiences and research outcomes on all aspects of construction materials and technologies including sustainable development. Changing technological needs in civil engineering all over the globe has led to an exponential growth in R&D in various domains such as structural engineering, composite structures, materials etc., The conference is expected to address all such recent and state of art R&D issues.
