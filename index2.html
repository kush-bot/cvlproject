ABOUT THE INSTITUTION

Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (SSIT), Tumakuru, Karnataka, one of the most sought after technical institutions in the country, was started in the year 1979 under the ambit of Sri Siddhartha Education Society (SSES), Tumakuru, Karnataka. It is just sixty km away from Bengaluru with lush green serene campus spread over fifty acres of the land and has the state of art infrastructural support. SSES was established in the year 1959 by Dr. H M Gangadharaiah with the blessings of Acharya Vinobha Bhave. It was started as one school and has grown consistently to be eighty four institutions now, spread all over the southern part of Karnataka. The academic empire includes an engineering college, two medical colleges, dental college, nursing college, first grade colleges, B.Ed. colleges, training institutes, Sanskrit and poly schools and a large number of high schools with the vision to “Impart education to reach the unreached”. SSIT is one of the constituent college under SSAHE, Tumakuru, a decade old Deemed-to- be university with NAAC-A grade. At present SSIT run ten UG programmes in Bachelor of Engineering (BE) (with six programmes are accredited by NBA), 07 programmes in Post Graduation (PG), Doctoral programme and MCA with a total of annual intake of around thousand students.

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The department was one of the two departments in SSIT to be started in 1979. The Current intake is sixty students for under graduate and eighteen students for post graduate in CAD structures, which was started in the year 2008. The department has well qualified, experienced and dedicated staff members with 6 PhD's. The department has well equipped laboratories required to train the students as per the syllabi. The department has been NBA accredited continuously from 2004 till date. Most recently department has been accredited for six years. The department has a widely sought after consultancy center, which ensures good internal revenue generation (IRG), catering to the needs of R&D establishments. The department has four professional body chapters ICI, INSTRUCT, ACCE, IGBC and MoU with leading industrial organizations which ensures industry institution interactions and boosts the industry- academia collaborations.
